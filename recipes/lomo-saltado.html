Lomo Saltado

Lomo Saltado is a traditional Peruvian dish from Arequipa

Ingredients

  • 2 oz nuts, toasted
  • 6 oz onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 4 oz Mirasol chili pepper (or 3 tablespoons of Mirasol chili pepper paste)
  • 2 tablespoons Ají Amarillo (or 2 tablespoons Ají Amarillo paste)

Directions

  1. Roast the onion and garlic in a clean saucepan. Reserve.
  2. Boil the ají Mirasol, changing the water three times. Peel and discard the seeds.
  3. In a blender process chili peppers, garlic, onion, peanuts, nuts, milks, queso fresco, soda crackers, huacatay leaves or paste. Season with salt.
  4. In a dish put lettuce leaves, potato slices, cover with the sauce, and decorate with hard-boiled eggs, white cheese, black olives.

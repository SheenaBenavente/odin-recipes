Emoliente

Emoliente is a traditional Peruvian drink and is known for its medicinal properties.

Ingredients

  • Dried horse tail
  • Dried grass
  • Flax seeds
  • Llanten (plantain leaf)
  • Chopped quince (optional)
  • Water
  • Sugar or honey
  • Lemon

Steps

  1. Boil all the ingredients in a large pan filled with water, for half an hour.
  2. Strain, sweeten, and add lemon juice to taste.
